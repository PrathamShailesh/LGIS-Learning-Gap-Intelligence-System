# Project Summary

## Project Name

Learning Gap Intelligence System (LGIS)

---

## Objective

The objective of LGIS is to help education administrators identify learning gaps and performance disparities using educational assessment data. The platform enables faster, evidence-based decision making through analytics dashboards and actionable insights.

---

## Problem

Educational performance data is often distributed through static reports, making it difficult for administrators to identify weak regions and respond quickly to emerging learning challenges.

As a result:

* Learning gaps remain unnoticed for extended periods.
* Resources may not be allocated effectively.
* Students continue to struggle without targeted intervention.

---

## Solution

LGIS converts raw educational datasets into an interactive intelligence platform.

The system provides:

* Performance monitoring dashboards
* State and grade comparisons
* Risk identification for low-performing regions
* AI-assisted recommendations
* Trend analysis and reporting

---

## Data Used

Source:

* ASER Educational Dataset

Dataset includes:

* Reading proficiency
* Mathematics proficiency
* State-wise performance
* Grade-wise performance
* Historical learning outcomes

---

## Analytics Performed

### Reading Performance Analysis

Evaluated reading proficiency across grades and states to identify strong and weak performers.

### Mathematics Performance Analysis

Analyzed mathematics outcomes and compared them against reading performance.

### State Performance Comparison

Compared educational outcomes between regions to identify disparities.

### Learning Gap Detection

Measured differences between expected and actual proficiency levels.

### Trend Monitoring

Observed changes in learning outcomes across time periods.

---

## AI Use Cases

### Risk Detection

Identify regions with consistently low educational outcomes.

### Recommendation Generation

Suggest intervention strategies based on performance patterns.

### Automated Reporting

Generate summaries and insights for education officers.

### Predictive Monitoring

Potential future capability to forecast educational performance trends.

LGIS enables:

* Faster identification of learning challenges

* Improved monitoring of student outcomes
